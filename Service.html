Giving to the community

Beta Beta Food Drive

As a member of the beta beta food drive which aims to make a positive impact on communties who are in need of food. Every friday, we visit neighboring communities who are in need by providing a truckload of canned and boxed food. We ensure that all families have access to nutritious and filling meals for the entire week. Not only do we provide meals, we also foster a community of people who enjoy helping others.
